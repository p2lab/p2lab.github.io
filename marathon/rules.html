Hey there, POKéTRAINERS! If you're here, I bet you're curious about the way I'll be playing the games:

  1. I will play AT LEAST one game of each generation (doesn't matter if it's a remake).
  2. The game will be marked as BEATEN after I've done everything post-game related.
  3. This is not a nuzlocke. It's just me playing the way I want.
  4. I will try to finish Pokémon XY by the time Sun/Moon are released. This doesn't mean it'll be the day before, it means I'll try to finish as close as possible to the release date.
  5. I'll update the main page whenever I see I've made significant progress.

The last game I'll be playing is Pokémon Sun/Moon. I think it's a funny way to celebrate #Pokémon20.
If you want to join this marathon, just let me know on Twitter! It's always great to hear more people are doing this :D
